Gameplay

In Wizard Adventure Saga, you paly as a wizard traveling through the land, with a quest to become to best wizard in the world. Equipped with 2 powerful spells, a lightning strike (Q), and an AOE ice blast(E). The AOE ability has high damage, yet high mana cost, and you cannot use it while moving, so be sure to use it wisely!

Boss

In Wizard Adventure Saga, you are straight away engaged in an intesnse battle with an artificial intelligent dragon, the boss of the game. The dragon is trying to prevent you from becoming the best wizard. To become the best wizard, you must defeat him. He delivers strong fire attacks, and will not let you win easily!
