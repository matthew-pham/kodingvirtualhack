About

Wizard Adventure Saga was created by 3 members of a team at the Koding Virtual Hackathon. It was submitted as a project in the 'Game' category. The creators are: Kevin Palani, Basit Ali, and Matthew Pham.


Kevin handeled the game engine, rendering engine, the AI engine, scheduling engine, and animator using Java.

Basit did the physics engine, audio engine, and animator.

Matthew designed and created the website, embeded the game, and created the login and registration services.
